# My Magic Library: Cute & Magical Design System

This document outlines the visual language for "My Magic Library" to create a playful, whimsical, and "cute" experience for users.

## 1. Visual Style: Claymorphism 2.0
We use **Claymorphism** as the primary UI style. It features soft 3D, chunky, and bubbly elements that feel tactile and toy-like.

- **Border Radius:** Large and rounded (16px to 32px).
- **Borders:** Thick, soft borders (3px to 4px) in slightly darker versions of the surface color.
- **Shadows:** Double shadow technique (inner + outer).
  - Outer: Soft, blurred shadow to give "lift".
  - Inner: One light (top-left) for "highlight" and one dark (bottom-right) for "depth".
- **Colors:** A palette of soft pastels.

## 3. Typography
- **Headings:** `Baloo 2` (Bold, rounded, very friendly).
- **Body:** `Comic Neue` (Legible but playful, feels like a storybook).

## 5. UI Components
- **Buttons:** Large, "squishy" looking buttons that scale down on click.
- **Cards:** White or very light pastel with thick colored borders and deep soft shadows.
- **Background:** Subtle radial gradients or playful patterns (dots, stars, clouds).
